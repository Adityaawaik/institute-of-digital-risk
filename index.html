Who We Are

Industry-led.
Academically grounded.

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute specialising in digital, cyber and technology risk roles across regulated, high-consequence environments. We bridge the gap between academic insight and the pressures of real-world risk management.

Through a close partnership with a leading UK university, IDR develops practitioners who can operate at the intersection of emerging technology, regulatory compliance, and organisational resilience — from day one of deployment.

Academic Collaboration

Curriculum co-designed with a UK university partner, ensuring intellectual rigour alongside vocational depth.

Industry Practice

Live project work, sector placements and employer-defined competency frameworks embedded throughout.

Regulated Environments

Focused on financial services, critical national infrastructure, and other high-consequence sectors.
